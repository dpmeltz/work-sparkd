Daniel P. Meltzer makes work better for people. Currently he does that by helping HR departments use data to make better _people_ focused decisions. His HR experience is primarily in employee surveys, talent management, and compensation. Dan holds a Ph.D. in Applied Organizational Psychology from Hofstra University where he now serves as an adjunct faculty member. Dan considers his specialty to be the creative application of motivational science to practice in all areas of HR.

Academically, his research revolves around understanding how people can beat work stress. His goal is to help employers embed experiences into the workplace that reduce the impact of various common workplace stresses. Dan is also a full time father, and husband, as well as a part time sourdough baker.
